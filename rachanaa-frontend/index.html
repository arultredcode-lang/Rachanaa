FAQ

Your Questions, Answered

What makes Rachanaa's insurance prices the best?
+
We are a multi-insurer IRDAI registered partner — meaning we compare quotes from 20+ insurance companies in real time and offer you the lowest available premium for the same coverage. You're never stuck with one insurer's price.
Is the entire process really 100% online?
+
Yes — completely. From initial consultation via WhatsApp or video call, to document submission, payment, and policy or loan disbursal — everything is handled digitally anywhere in India.
Do you serve outside Kollegal?
+
Absolutely. While we're based in Kollegal, Karnataka, we serve clients Pan India — including Chamarajanagar, Mysuru, Mandya, Nanjangud, Gundlupet, Bangalore and all states across India. Everything is 100% online.
How do education loans abroad work?
+
We facilitate education loans for students going to USA, UK, Canada, Australia, Germany and more. Loans cover tuition, accommodation, living expenses. Both collateral and non-collateral options are available through our NBFC partners (InCred, Credila, Avanse, Auxilo) with visa-linked disbursal.
Is there a fee for aviation career placement?
+
Zero placement fee for candidates. We earn only from our employer partners — airlines, airports, and ground handling agencies. This means our incentive is to genuinely find you the best placement opportunity with our 15+ airline partners.
Are there any hidden charges?
+
Zero hidden charges. All applicable fees — processing fees, premiums, interest rates — are clearly communicated before you sign anything. We believe in full transparency because your trust is our business. This is our commitment to every client across India.
